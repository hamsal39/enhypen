This is a brief biography of the South Korean boy group, ENHYPEN.

Hi there! You must be an ENGENE! Let's explore the journey of the seven members who emerged from the survival show I-LAND.

ENHYPEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Belift Lab. The group consists of seven members: Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. They debuted on November 30, 2020, with their mini-album "Border: Day One."

Heeseung: The Center & Ace

Heeseung, whose real name is Lee Heeseung, is the eldest member of BTS. Born on October 15, 2001, in Namyangju, South Korea.

He is known for his perfect pitch and being a legendary trainee who excels in vocals, dance, and rap.

"If you work hard, the results will follow."

Jay: The Charismatic Rapper

Jay, whose real name is Park Jongseong, was born on April 20, 2002, in Seattle, Washington, USA.

He is known for his honesty, passion for fashion, and his powerful stage presence.

"I want to be a person who can give strength to others."

Jake: The Icon of Growth

Jake, whose real name is Sim Jaeyun, was born on November 15, 2002, in Brisbane, Australia.

He is known for his positive energy, his "golden retriever" personality, and his rapid improvement since I-LAND.

"Enjoy the process, even if it's hard."

Sunghoon: The Ice Prince

Sunghoon, whose real name is Park Sunghoon, was born on December 8, 2002, in Namyangju, South Korea.

Before his idol career, he was an award-winning competitive figure skater, known for his elegance and visuals.

"I want to show a side of me that is constantly evolving."

Sunoo: The Master of Duality

Sunoo, whose real name is Kim Sunoo, was born on June 24, 2003, in Suwon, South Korea.

He is famous for his bright "sunshine" personality, his incredible facial expressions, and his unique vocal color.

"I want to be an artist who can provide comfort to people."

Jungwon: The Leader

Jungwon, whose real name is Yang Jungwon, was born on February 9, 2004, in Seoul, South Korea.

He is the group's reliable leader, respected for his maturity, hidden strength, and sharp dance moves.

"I will become a leader who doesn't lose his initial mindset."

Ni-ki: The Dance Prodigy

Ni-ki, whose real name is Nishimura Riki, was born on December 9, 2005, in Okayama, Japan.

As the youngest member (Maknae), he is widely considered one of the best dancers of his generation.

"I want to become a world-class artist."

The Legacy

With seven members—Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki—ENHYPEN continues to bridge the gap between their origins and their future. Their bond with "ENGENE" remains the heart of their journey across the globe.
